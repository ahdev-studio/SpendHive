We do not share your personal information with third parties except as described in this Privacy Policy. We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers that perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting services.

We use reasonable measures to help protect your personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. All data you enter is stored securely.
