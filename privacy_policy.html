Privacy Policy for SpendHive

Last Updated: June 18, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to SpendHive ("we," "our," "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application, SpendHive.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Data You Provide: All financial data, such as income, expenses, and categories, that you manually enter into the app.
  • Google User Information: When you choose to back up your data, we use Google Sign-In to authenticate your identity. We receive your basic profile information (like email address) for identification purposes only.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the app's functionality:

  • To store and display your financial records on your device.
  • To enable the data backup and restore feature via your personal Google Drive. The app creates a private folder in your Google Drive that only SpendHive can access. We do not access, view, or interact with any other files or folders in your Google Drive.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your financial data is stored locally on your device. When you use the backup feature, an encrypted backup file is stored in a special "app data" folder on your Google Drive, which is protected by Google's security protocols.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal data with any third parties.
